# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project exists to help immigrants — one of the most vulnerable groups in any society. Everyone who contributes, whether they are writing code, translating, or correcting a fact, shares that mission.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, immigration status, level of experience, nationality, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**We welcome:**
- Respectful, constructive feedback
- Translations, corrections, and lived experience from immigrants
- Questions from first-time contributors
- Disagreement expressed with kindness

**We do not welcome:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Political statements unrelated to the tools we build
- Sharing others' personal immigration stories without consent
- Any content that discriminates based on nationality, ethnicity, or immigration status

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to **7feilee@gmail.com**. All reports are confidential. Maintainers will respond within 48 hours.

Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity.
